Internet Treatment

Starting a traditional therapy can sometimes be a big step and can be a difficult step to take. A smaller step can be to start a treatment via the internet. For those who have family and work, it can often be difficult to find the time and peace to start a traditional treatment. At Vicente, you can easily book, pay and carry out your desired therapy treatment from home
