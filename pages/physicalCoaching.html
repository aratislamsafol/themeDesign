Psychological Coaching

Reasons for going to therapy can be different, you want to feel better, you want to get to know yourself, work on a problem you have or solve an acute crisis. Maybe you need help to get on in life and it may no longer be enough to talk to family, friends or other relatives. It may be difficult for you to talk openly about problems with those you know.

Life has hit you and things have happened that make you anxious or depressed. For example, if you have problems at work or in a relationship, it can lead to you ending up in a long-term depression or in an acute crisis.

You may have questions about the meaning of life or something else that feels difficult. Sometimes you can also feel fear of things without any logical explanation for it and you feel bad without knowing exactly why.

Going to therapy can be a way to deepen your knowledge of how you think, feel and act. Learning more about yourself often makes you feel better about yourself and feel more secure. It can also help you to change a behavior or to distance yourself from negative and destructive thought patterns.

The therapy is always based on you and your needs. You choose what you want to process. I offer therapy in Stockholm for individuals, couples and groups.
